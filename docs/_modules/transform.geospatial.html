transform.geospatial.divide_bbox_amsterdam_in_quadrants module¶

transform.geospatial.divide_bbox_amsterdam_in_quadrants.calculation(number_of_boxes, bbox)¶

Divide the BBOX of the City of Amsterdam in x number of quadrants/rectangles for use in limiting WFS/geo queries. Args:

  1. number_of_boxes: in multiples of 2, 8 works well for most cases.
  2. bbox: [110200,476772,134030,493900]
Results:
list of quadrants [[x1,y1,x2,y2],[..]]

transform.geospatial.get_areacodes_from_latlon.getAreaCodes(item, lat, lon)¶

ex: https://api.data.amsterdam.nl/geosearch/search/?item=verblijfsobject&lat=52.3731750&lon=4.8924655&radius=50

transform.geospatial.rd_to_wgs84.rd_to_wgs84(X, Y)¶
